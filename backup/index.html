About RDF

Rainbow Dhamma Foundation is a Section 8 Non-Profit Organization dedicated to fostering peace, inclusivity, and holistic well-being.

We bridge the gap between ancient wisdom and modern compassionate living. Our mission is to serve all beings with equanimity and kindness, irrespective of their background.
